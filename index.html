About

Feeling like reading Version

I’m currently looking for a Mid. UI position at a company, where I can learn, stretch my knowledge and strengthen my weaknesses. I want to help build great, meaningful and easy-to-use experiences, applications and interactions to make people’s life easier and business succeed.

My design career started in 2007 as a Graphic Designer in an advertising agency in Brazil where I had the opportunity to work with well known and exciting brands such as Google, Sony Pictures and Disney and work in a variety of challenging projects. After 5 years I flew over the sea for my first international experience. Currently, I’m working as a Digital Creative Director in an Creative Agency in Dublin - Ireland. It’s been amazing working in so many projects, different culture and markets, learn a whole bunch of skills, and most important meet so many talent people.

What I most love about Design is making the right questions, searching for answers for problems people don’t even notice they have, and putting user and possible solutions in context and test them all. And when someone else would consider the job done, you go there and start again.

Other interests include woodworking, photography, music, and 3d modelling. Among thinks I’m interest to dive more deeply are home automation (IoT, Raspberry Pi, Arduino) and 3D print. I love eggs for breakfast and dislike sweet mixed with savoury food (not that sophisticated huh?).
